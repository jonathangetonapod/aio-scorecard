🎯 How This Works

🚨 The Problem: AI is Replacing Google

B2B buyers are changing how they research. Instead of scrolling through Google results, they're asking AI directly:

  • "Who are the best CNC machining companies in Denver?"
  • "I need a quote for injection molding services"
  • "Compare the top aerospace manufacturers near me"

If AI doesn't mention you, you're invisible to these buyers. They'll never see your website, never request a quote, never become a customer.

🔬 Our Testing Methodology

🤖 OpenAI GPT-5 The most widely used AI assistant (ChatGPT)

  • Supplier queries: "Who are the best [specific capability] manufacturers?"
  • Quote queries: "I need a quote for [specific capability] services"

📉 Why Most Scores Are Low

Don't be alarmed if you score under 20% — that's typical. Here's why:

  • AI models are trained on web content, and most B2B sites aren't optimized for AI
  • AI prefers authoritative sources: directories, news mentions, case studies
  • Local businesses are harder for AI to discover than national brands

The good news? Your competitors probably score low too. This is a new channel — the companies that optimize first will capture the market.

🛠️ How to Improve Your Score

AI visibility is trainable. Here's what works:

  • Website Content: Use the exact language buyers search for (specific capabilities, processes, materials)
  • Published Authority: Case studies, guides, and FAQs that AI can cite as sources
  • Digital Footprint: Industry directories, Google Business Profile, customer reviews, press mentions
  • Structured Data: Schema markup that helps AI understand your services
